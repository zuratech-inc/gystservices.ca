## About This Workshop

This interactive workshop invites participants from all cultural backgrounds to explore how cultural identity and traditional practices contribute to mental wellness. Through guided exercises, group discussion, and reflective activities, participants will discover how reconnecting with cultural roots can serve as a powerful foundation for emotional resilience.

## What to Expect

- Guided group exercises exploring cultural identity and wellness
- Open discussion in a safe, facilitated environment
- Reflective journaling prompts to take home
- Light refreshments provided

## Who Is This For?

Everyone is welcome regardless of background. Whether you have a strong connection to your cultural heritage or are just beginning to explore it, this workshop meets you where you are.

> When life gets complicated, as it often does, I have learned to simplify by returning to core values. This workshop helps you discover yours.
