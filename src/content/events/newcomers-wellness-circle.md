## About This Circle

Moving to a new place brings excitement and challenge in equal measure. This gentle, guided sharing circle creates space for newcomers to share their experiences, process the emotional dimensions of transition, and build meaningful connections with others on a similar path.

## What to Expect

- A warm welcome and introductions in a judgement-free space
- Guided sharing prompts (no pressure to speak)
- Active listening and mutual support from the group
- Connection with others who understand the journey of transition

## Who Is This For?

Anyone who has recently moved to a new community and is navigating the emotional side of that transition. No prior experience with group work is needed. All cultural backgrounds are warmly welcome.

## Circle Guidelines

Speak from the heart. Listen without judgment. Take only the time you need. What is shared in circle stays in circle.
