## About This Seminar

Drawing from Family Systems Therapy, this educational seminar explores how the families we grow up in shape our beliefs, behaviours, and relationships. Participants will learn about intergenerational patterns, family roles, and practical strategies for building healthier dynamics.

## Topics Covered

- How family systems shape individual behaviour across generations
- Understanding intergenerational patterns, rules, and roles
- Recognizing unhealthy dynamics and their cultural nuances
- Practical strategies for creating positive change in your system

## Format

The evening begins with a 45-minute presentation by Valerie, followed by small group discussion and a Q&A session. A take-home resource guide is included for all participants.

## No Prior Knowledge Needed

This seminar is designed to be accessible to everyone. Whether you have a background in psychology or are encountering these ideas for the first time, you will find value here.
