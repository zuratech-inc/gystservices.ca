approach:
  label: Therapeutic Perspective
  heading: Grounded in culture, guided by theory
  body: My therapeutic perspective is influenced at its foundation by cultural teachings and integrates three evidence-based frameworks.
  cards:
    - title: Existentialism
      body: A philosophical approach at whose core is the belief that each of us has the ability to find meaning in our lives. The counsellor works to understand you on your own subjective terms, encouraging reflection and taking responsibility for change.
    - title: Solutions-Focused Brief Therapy
      body: Concentrating on small, realistic, and sustainable goals that result in positive outcomes. You are empowered as the expert in who you are — we focus on solutions and results rather than dwelling on problems.
    - title: Family Systems Therapy
      body: Focusing on human problems from a relational perspective — we are best understood by how we interact within our systems. This explores intergenerational meanings, rules, processes, and cultural nuances.
